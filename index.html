How to Get Your Mobile App -Step by Step

You can have your own app in 3 simple steps.

1.Request a Trial for your Mobile App

Access the Create Your App page and fill out the form.Kingly input your organization's website adress, contact information, and your mobile app's desired features
Once you've submitted the form, you'll be contacted within 3 business days by our development team to start your mobile app.

2.App Management Training

At any time during your 90 days trial, you can request an app management training with one of our sonsultants.
Tha training will be conducted over a call by our dedicated app administator.
You will learn how to set up your app, how to start adding content and how to keep your audience engaged.

3.Google play and iTunes Submission

Once you've tested out the mobile app and you're ready to purchase, you'll have to move to a paid account. This is whre we'll submit your app.
We'll need around 3-5 business days to prepare your app for submission. We'll have to wait around 7-15 business days for iTunes and 1-3 days for Google Play approval.
